Andar Bahar

A simple game of chance to predict which box has the first card or Joker, Andar Bahar is a game of pure luck.

How to Play Andar Bahar

  • The dealer shuffles the 52-card deck and places one card facing up in front of you and other players on the table.
  • You will have to place the bet on one of the two piles that are inside (Andar) and outside (Bahar).
  • Until the dealer announces the closing of bets, you can keep placing or modifying them as you please. The bets close when the dealer deals the Joker card.
  • If you placed your bet Andar and the card with the same value is dealt as Andar, you win. Same goes for Bahar.

In Andar Bahar, the card values or rank do not matter. The side card (s) must match the Joker.

Teen Patti

Popular all over the Indian subcontinent, Teen Patti, aka, Flush or Flash, starts with placing a bet on a deck of cards without Joker. The player can raise the bet by adding cash or making a call.

How to Play Teen Patti

  • After shuffling the 52-card deck minus the Joker, the player places the first bet, known as the ‘Ante’. Ante is the compulsory bet that each player must place at the beginning of every round.
  • The dealer deals three cards facing down for themselves, and three cards each for each player, clockwise.
  • If you get three consecutive cards from the same suit, it is a straight flush. The highest rank of cards in a straight flush are A, 2 and 3. The lowest rank comprises 4,3,2.
  • You can win even with three of the same cards from different suits. A is the highest rank, and 2 is the lowest.
  • A normal run is another winning option on the table where you have different suits but cards with consecutive numbers. Here the A is ranked highest and 2 lowest.
  • Even when you have three cards of the same suit and not in any order, you can win.
  • Two cards of the same rank but from different suits will let you win too, if other players do not have the rest.

If you are not able to join this game while in progress, simply wait for the round to end and then begin!

Roulette

Here the player has to predict where the ball will land on the spinning wheel with 37 numbers.

How to Play Roulette

  • Before the dealer spins the wheel, place the bet.
  • If you bet on the colour and number where the ball lands, you win.
  • Keep in mind about inside and outside bets. Inside bets are the numbers from 1 to 36 and have high payouts. Whereas outside bets are placed on colours, odd and even numbers, dozen (vertically), and columns, with relatively lower payouts.

One of the best strategies to win is to start by betting small or going for odd-even numbers.

Blackjack

The goal is to beat the dealer by drawing a hand above the value they have without exceeding 21. It is often the choicest game in the best live casinos in India like Big Boost and similar.

How to Play Blackjack

  • The dealer places two cards for themselves and two cards each for the players. One of the dealer’s cards faces down, while all the players’ cards are facing up.
  • One of the important things to remember is that numbers from 2 to 9 are ranked as numbers. Jack, Queen, and King are ranked as 10. A can be 1 or 11.
  • You and other players have the choice to draw more or no cards. Drawing more cards is called a ‘hit’ and choosing not to draw anymore is called a ‘stand’.
  • Make sure that your total does not exceed 21, which is known as a ‘bust’.

You are competing with the house or the dealer when you play Blackjack, and the game allows up to 7 players on the table.

Baccarat

Consisting of the player and banker with two cards for each, winning in Baccarat is to have a hand that is close to nine.

How to Play Baccarat

  • 10 and all face cards have a value of 0 in Baccarat. Ace has the value of 1.
  • If you score more than 9, the total will be deducted by the value of 10.
  • When you score a total of two cards that are between 8 and 9, you do not have to draw the third card.

It is most apt to bet on the banker’s (dealer’s) hand while playing Baccarat.
